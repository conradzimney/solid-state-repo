Data Analysis and Visualization

Unleashing the power of data through analysis and visualization

Experience

As an Intern Consultant on the Advanced Analytics team at West Monroe Partners, I had the opportunity to work on a wide variety of client projects such as data assessment and warehousing, custom web development, and building data analysis and reporting tools. This opportunity prompted my interest in data analysis, and showed me the power of telling stories through data visualization. I primarily used the Microsoft stack for data analysis and reporting, including Excel, PowerPivot, SQL, and SSRS. For visualization components, I used Tableau most frequently at West Monroe Partners, while for personal or acadmenic projects I used D3.js and jQuery, or custom visualization methods.

Living in a world with ever-increasing amounts of data, makes data analysis a critical skill that helps me immensely with my everyday development work. I am extremely interested in the power and capabilities of data science and machine learning, and greatly look forward to future advances in these areas.
